School Picker

College is a big deal nowadays, but I've noticed that there's a dearth of websites that allow a prospective college student to sort colleges by price, as well as student satisfaction. This inspired me to create this website with a simple UI to help teens and parents choose which college best suits their needs

Here is the list of schools

While implementing this project, I noticed there are many features that could be improved. For example:

  • Storing the schools in an actual database, instead of having the entire data in my javascript file
  • If the schools were in a database, I could use the auto-increment attribute of SQL (applying it to the school's ranking) to easily add a new school
  • Right now my student statisfaction column is based upon a school's freshman retention rate (i.e. the percentage of freshman who return for their sophomore year). Obviously this isn't the best indicator of a school's student statisfaction, as a freshman may not return for a variety of reasons (from lack of money to family issues). This shows that a better way of measuring student satisfaction, perhaps by taking in student input on a variety of topics (stress level, accessiblity of campus health, balance of social and school life) is needed.
  • To turn this into a full-fledged web applicaton, I could have a username and login (and store that in a database), have users write reviews for each school, etc.
